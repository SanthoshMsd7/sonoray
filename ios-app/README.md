# 📱 Sonoray ERP - iOS App Setup & Deployment Guide

This folder contains the complete native iOS source files matching our Android application's features (a high-performance `WKWebView` loading the ERP frontend, with a native Swift background tracking engine running `CLLocationManager`).

Because Xcode project generation is machine-specific (handling certificates, profiles, and localized UUIDs), follow these simple steps to import these files into Xcode on a Mac and build your app for free.

---

## 🛠️ Step 1: Create a New Xcode Project on Your Mac

1. Open **Xcode** on your Mac.
2. Select **File > New > Project...**
3. Choose **iOS** as the platform, and **App** as the template. Click **Next**.
4. Configure the project:
   - **Product Name:** `SonorayERP`
   - **Organization Identifier:** `com.sonoray`
   - **Bundle Identifier:** `com.sonoray.erp`
   - **Interface:** `Storyboard`
   - **Language:** `Swift`
5. Click **Next** and save the project inside a folder.

---

## 🚀 Step 2: Import the Source Files

Replace the boilerplate files generated by Xcode with the custom files in this folder:

1. Drag and drop the following files from your `ios-app/SonorayERP` folder into Xcode's left sidebar (inside the `SonorayERP` group):
   - `LocationManager.swift`
   - `ViewController.swift`
   - `AppDelegate.swift` (overwrite the existing one)
   - `SceneDelegate.swift` (overwrite the existing one)
2. Open your project's **`Info.plist`** in Xcode (or edit it as Source Code) and add the following keys to declare usage permissions (they are already set in our custom [Info.plist](file:///c:/Users/santh/.gemini/antigravity-ide/scratch/Uss/ios-app/SonorayERP/Info.plist)):
   - `NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription` (When In Use Location Prompt)
   - `NSLocationAlwaysAndWhenInUseUsageDescription` (Always Allow Location Prompt - **Required for background updates**)
   - `NSCameraUsageDescription` (Camera Upload Access)

---

## 🔋 Step 3: Enable Background Location Capability

Apple restricts apps from accessing location services in the background unless the specific background capability is checked:

1. Click on the root **`SonorayERP`** project node in Xcode's left sidebar.
2. Select the **`Signing & Capabilities`** tab at the top.
3. Click the **`+ Capability`** button in the top left.
4. Search for **`Background Modes`** and double-click to add it.
5. In the checklist of Background Modes, check **`Location updates`**.

---

## 📲 Step 4: Run the App on Your Phone (Free Personal Development)

You do **not** need a paid Apple Developer Account to test the app on your physical iPhone:

1. Connect your iPhone to your Mac via USB.
2. Select your device from the target list at the top of Xcode.
3. Go to the **`Signing & Capabilities`** tab.
4. Check **`Automatically manage signing`**.
5. Log in with your personal Apple ID (iCloud email) in the **`Team`** selector.
6. Click the **Run (Play)** button.
7. **First-time configuration on your iPhone**:
   - Tap **Settings > General > VPN & Device Management** on your iPhone.
   - Tap your developer profile and choose **Trust**.
   - Enable **Developer Mode** on your iPhone if running iOS 16+ (**Settings > Privacy & Security > Developer Mode**).
8. Re-run the app, and you're ready! It will track location in real-time even when you swipe the app closed!
